Pritong Manok Recipe

Fried Chicken, the Filipino Way

Pritong manok is everyones favorite! Thereâ€™s just nothing more delicious than this crispy and juicy fried chicken! Everyone in the family can never say no to this all-time favorite dish specially for Noche Buena!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Stalk Leeks
  • Star Anise
  • Whole Peppercorn
  • Chicken Stock
  • Laurel Leaves
  • Oil
  • Banana Ketchup

Steps

  1. Rub salt all over the chicken. Let it stay for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat the oil in a cooking pot.
  3. Combine all of the ingredients.
  4. Cook the "manok" or chicken until brown or golden
  5. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
